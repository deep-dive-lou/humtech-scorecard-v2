# Revenue Waterfall — How the Numbers Work

Quick reference for explaining the waterfall figures to leads or partners.

---

## The Three Stages

The waterfall measures recoverable revenue at three points in the sales funnel:

1. **Engagement** — What % of leads book an appointment/call?
2. **Attendance** — What % of booked leads actually show up?
3. **Conversion** — What % of attended meetings result in a sale?

---

## Industry Benchmarks

We compare the lead's actual rates against these benchmarks:

| Stage | Benchmark | Source |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Booking rate | 70% | Industry-standard conversion benchmarks |
| Attendance rate | 85% | Industry-standard conversion benchmarks |
| Conversion rate | 50% | Industry-standard conversion benchmarks |

If a company is already at or above the benchmark at any stage, that stage shows £0 recoverable — they're ahead of the curve there.

---

## How Each Figure is Calculated

**Recoverable = (Benchmark% - Their%) x Leads at that stage x Deal value**

### Worked Example

**Inputs:** 100 leads/month, £2,000 average deal value, 55% booking, 60% attendance, 30% conversion

**Stage 1 — Engagement gap:**
- Their booking rate: 55%, Benchmark: 70%
- Gap: 70% - 55% = 15%
- Leads affected: 100 (all inbound leads)
- Monthly recoverable: 100 x 15% x £2,000 = **£30,000/month**

**Stage 2 — Attendance gap:**
- Their attendance rate: 60%, Benchmark: 85%
- Gap: 85% - 60% = 25%
- Leads affected: 55 (the leads that actually booked — 100 x 55%)
- Monthly recoverable: 55 x 25% x £2,000 = **£27,500/month**

**Stage 3 — Conversion gap:**
- Their conversion rate: 30%, Benchmark: 50%
- Gap: 50% - 30% = 20%
- Leads affected: 33 (the leads that showed — 55 x 60%)
- Monthly recoverable: 33 x 20% x £2,000 = **£13,200/month**

**Total monthly recoverable:** £30,000 + £27,500 + £13,200 = **£70,700/month**
**Annual recoverable:** £70,700 x 12 = **£848,400/year**

---

## Key Points for Conversations

- **These are NOT theoretical losses.** They represent the gap between where you are and where the industry sits. We're not comparing against perfection.

- **Each stage is independent.** Fixing just one stage would recover that stage's figure — you don't need to fix all three to see results.

- **The figures use the lead's own numbers.** Lead volume, deal value, and conversion rates all come from what they told us. If they selected "I don't know", we use conservative industry defaults and flag it.

- **All figures are annual** (monthly x 12) for impact.

- The "Capture Rate" is the actual percentage of total pipeline they're currently closing: (leads x booking% x attendance% x conversion%) / total leads.

---

## Disclaimer (shown on every report)

> These figures represent recoverable revenue based on industry-standard conversion benchmarks of 70% booking, 85% attendance, and 50% conversion.
